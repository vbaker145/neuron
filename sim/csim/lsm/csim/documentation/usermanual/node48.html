CaChannel_Yamada98

From: Yamada, Koch and Adams: Multiple Channels and Calcium Dynamics, Editors: Koch, Segev, MIT Press 1998) Ca mechanism is included in the channel class not the neuron class.

Read/writable Fields

u (Mol) :
Constant step increase in the calcium concentration
Ts (Sec) :
Time constant for the deactivation of the calcium concentration
Ca (Mol) :
Interior calcium concentration
Gbar (S) :
The maximum conductance of the channel;
Erev (V) :
The reversal potential Erev of the channel

Readonly Fields

g (S) :
The coductance g(t,Vm) of the channel
