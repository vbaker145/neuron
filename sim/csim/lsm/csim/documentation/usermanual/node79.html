KChannel_Korngreen02

Uses KnGate_Korngreen02 and KlGate_Korngreen02

Reference: Korngreen A, Sakmann B.Voltage-gated K+ channels in layer 5 neocortical pyramidal neurones from young rats: subtypes and gradients. J Physiol. 2000 Jun 15;525 Pt 3:621-39.

Read/writable Fields

Ts :
Scale of the time constants of the gating variables
Gbar (S) :
The maximum conductance of the channel;
Erev (V) :
The reversal potential Erev of the channel

Readonly Fields

g (S) :
The coductance g(t,Vm) of the channel
