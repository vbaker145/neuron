AHP_Channel

Reference: Fuhrmann G, Markram H, Tsodyks M. Spike frequency adaptation and neocortical rhythms. J Neurophysiol. 2002 Aug;88(2):761-70.

Read/writable Fields

Gbar (S) :
The maximum conductance of the channel;
u :
Constant step increase in n for each spike;
Ts :
Time constant for the deactivation of the current;
Erev (V) :
The reversal potential Erev of the channel

Readonly Fields

n :
Fraction of the open conductance;
g (S) :
The coductance g(t,Vm) of the channel
