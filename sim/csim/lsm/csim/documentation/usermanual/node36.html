AChannel_Hoffman97

Uses AmGate_Hoffman97 and AhGate_Hoffman97

Reference: Hoffman, D., Magee, J.C., Colbert, C.M., and Johnston, D. Potassium channel regulation of signal propagation in dendrites of hippocampal pyramidal neurons. Nature 387:869-875 (1997)

Read/writable Fields

Gbar (S) :
The maximum conductance of the channel;
Erev (V) :
The reversal potential Erev of the channel

Readonly Fields

g (S) :
The coductance g(t,Vm) of the channel
