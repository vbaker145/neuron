Traubs_HH_Na_Channel

Used in the TraubsHHNeuron neuron model.

Uses the gates Traubs_HH_h_Gate and Traubs_HH_m_Gate.

gNa = 0.1 S/cm2 ENa = 50 mV

The maximum conductance of the channel;
Erev (V) :
The reversal potential Erev of the channel

Readonly Fields
